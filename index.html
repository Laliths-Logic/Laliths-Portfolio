About Me

Behind the
lens & timeline

I'm Lalith, a Chennai-based creative originally from Madurai, currently pursuing B.Tech Information Technology at Rajalakshmi Engineering College. My passion for photography, videography, and editing started as a personal interest and evolved into freelance creative work during my second year.

Being part of my college photography club gave me real event experience — covering major culturals and tech fests including Recharge and Titanium, where I photographed personalities including A.R. Rahman, Kollywood artists, singers, and industry guests.

My style combines clean visuals, cinematic framing, and storytelling-focused editing. I enjoy teamwork, fast-paced productions, and organizing creative workflows during live event coverage.

How I Work

The creative
process

01

Brief & Vision

We start with a conversation about your project — the event, the story, the mood you want to capture. Understanding the vision shapes every creative decision.

02

Pre-Production

Shot plans, gear setup, schedule coordination, and location walk-through. Fast-paced events demand preparation — so nothing important is missed on the day.

03

Shoot & Capture

On-ground with the Nikon ecosystem — capturing live performances, candid moments, stage energy, and behind-the-scenes authenticity as it happens.

04

Edit & Deliver

Color grading, sound design, precise cuts, and cinematic pacing — in Adobe Premiere Pro — until the final output tells the story exactly as it felt in the room.
